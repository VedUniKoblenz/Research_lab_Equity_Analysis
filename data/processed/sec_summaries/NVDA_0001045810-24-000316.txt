Even more critical, four customers represent **50%** of the total **$14.7B** Accounts Receivable balance, creating high sensitivity to CSP capital expenditure cycles.

• **Segment KPI Trajectory:** Compute & Networking revenue hit **$30.7B** (87.5% of total), driven by a shift in Data Center mix toward networking; Ethernet-for-AI (Spectrum-X) is emerging as the primary alpha driver within the segment as GPU demand hits supply ceilings.
