• **Haleon Liquidity Pivot:** Aggressive monetization of equity method investments; reduced ownership from 32% to 22.6% in March 2024, generating $3.1B in gross proceeds. Signals prioritized deleveraging over long-term dividend income from consumer health.
• **Restructuring Velocity:** $1.1B in restructuring/implementation charges recognized in Q1 2024 alone under the "Realigning Our Cost Base" program. Management is front-loading 27.5% of the $4.0B FY2024 savings target in 90 days, indicating higher-than-telegraphed urgency in headcount/operational reduction.
• **Inventory Margin Drag:** Amortization of Seagen-related inventory fair-value step-up contributed to significant COGS pressure; $577M recognized in Q1. Expected persistence of non-cash margin compression as Seagen legacy stock flushes through the system.
• **COVID Erosion Floor:** Comirnaty revenue collapsed 88% to $354M; Paxlovid down 50% to $2.0B. Total COVID-19 franchise now represents only 16% of total revenue, shifting the alpha thesis entirely to the $12.5B non-COVID base (11% operational growth).
• **Debt/Interest Headwind:** Interest expense, net, surged to $1.1B in Q1 2024 from $226M in Q1 2023. The $31B debt issuance for Seagen is creating a massive 400%+ YoY drag on net income.
• **Intangible Amortization Spike:** Amortization of finite-lived intangible assets hit $1.2B in Q1 (primarily Seagen developed technology rights), a permanent higher-plateau expense versus pre-acquisition levels.
• **Oncology KPI Shift:** Post-Seagen, Oncology now accounts for 24% of total revenue ($3.5B). Key performance indicators shifted to Padcev and Adcetris integration, which contributed significantly to the 11% non-COVID operational growth.
• **Capital Allocation Stasis:** Zero share repurchases in Q1 2024; primary capital utility remains $0.42/share dividend and debt retirement, confirming a multi-quarter pause on buyback-driven EPS support.
