• **Guidance Alpha:** FY24 revenue guidance raised by $2.0B to $42.4B–$43.6B; management expresses "high confidence" in incretin demand, yet Trulicity collapsed 26% YoY to $1.46B, signaling a forced transition where supply constraints are dictating product mix rather than organic market demand.
• **Inventory Scaling:** Inventory surged 16.3% in 90 days to $6.21B (vs $5.34B at FYE23); specifically, Work-In-Progress (WIP) and raw materials for GLP-1/GIP production are being aggressively front-loaded ahead of projected H2 manufacturing capacity expansion.
• **Operational Pivot:** R&D intensity increased to 23.4% of revenue ($2.52B, +27% YoY), focused on the "Triple G" (retatrutide) and oral GLP-1 (orforglipron) programs, marking a definitive shift from legacy insulin maintenance to metabolic dominance. 
• **Margin Dynamics:** Gross margin expanded 430bps to 80.9%, driven by favorable product mix (Verzenio +40% to $1.05B; Mounjaro US +124% volume), successfully offsetting a -4% net price headwind in the US Trulicity segment.
• **Capital Allocation:** Reprioritized $110.5M into IPR&D via the NEX-20 acquisition and other bolt-ons; dividend maintained at $1.30/share, but cash and equivalents dropped 21% to $2.27B as CAPEX for manufacturing sites (Indiana, North Carolina, Ireland, Germany) accelerates.
• **Competitive/Regulatory Risk:** New emphasis on "supply-induced" volume volatility; management's tactical use of "we anticipate" regarding production timing suggests localized bottlenecks in parenteral filling lines could lead to quarterly revenue "lumpiness" despite raised annual targets.
