• **Operational Pivot:** Initiation of a secondary "2024 Restructuring Plan" layering upon the existing 2023 Plan. Q2 2025 activity shows a shift from headcount reduction to "Facility Closing" charges, signaling a more permanent rationalization of the physical footprint following the 2023 severance cycle.
• **Asset Perimeter Redefinition:** Formal classification of significant assets as "Discontinued Operations Held for Sale" as of June 30, 2025. This confirms a definitive strategic exit from the Fiber/Small Cell segment, moving beyond "strategic review" to active divestiture status. 
• **Capital Structure Inflation:** Substantial shift in weighted average cost of debt. New issuances—specifically the 5.600% (Dec 2023) and 5.200% (Aug 2024) Senior Unsecured Notes—are replacing legacy maturities in the 1.350%–3.300% range (e.g., April/June 2020 Notes). Interest expense headwind is accelerating as the $20B+ unsecured stack reprices.
• **Guidance Nuance:** Management shift to "anticipates" regarding tower organic growth versus "expects" for core site rental revenue, suggesting moderate confidence in carrier 5G overlay pace but high confidence in existing contract escalators.
• **KPI Trajectory:** Tower segment shows sustained site rental growth, but Discontinued Operations (Fiber) metrics are being deprecated, masking underlying churn in the enterprise fiber sub-segment. 
• **Capital Allocation:** Dividend payout of $1.565/share maintained through Q2 2025, but the 2024 Restructuring Plan and asset sale "Held for Sale" designation suggest a potential re-baselining of the dividend post-divestiture to align with a pure-play tower REIT payout ratio.
