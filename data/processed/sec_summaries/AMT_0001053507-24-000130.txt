• **Oceania Divestiture:** Classified ATC Australia and ATC New Zealand as assets held for sale; full exit from Oceania marks a strategic retreat from peripheral markets to focus on high-density US/Europe/Data Center assets.
• **Europe Restructuring:** Initiated structural pivots in Germany and Spain (Note 1); restructuring charges signal a shift from inorganic expansion to operational efficiency/margin protection in mature European markets.
• **Data Center Capital Structure:** Utilization of Stonepeak Development Partnership and issuance of Preferred Stock in Data Center subsidiaries; indicates heavy reliance on external private equity to fund CoreSite expansion without further diluting AMT common equity.
• **KPI Trajectory:** Data Center segment NRR/Backlog remains high-conviction; US/Canada organic tenant billings growth (OTBG) remains ~5.0% despite Sprint churn overhang; Latin America revenue impacted by specific entity restructuring.
• **Geographic Risk Escalation:** New explicit concentration risk disclosures for Ghana (GH) regarding both revenue and asset base, signaling heightened macroeconomic vulnerability in West African operations.
• **Capital Allocation:** Maintained consistent dividend growth despite India impairment; debt profile managed through new senior notes (2027-2034) to lock in rates ahead of anticipated 2025 maturities.
