• **Cross-Border Alpha:** Cross-border volume remains the primary growth catalyst, up 35% on a local currency basis, significantly outperforming switched transaction growth of 12%.

• **Debt/Refinancing Nuance:** Issued $1.5B in senior notes in March 2023 at significantly higher coupons (4.85% and 4.875%) compared to maturing tranches, signaling a transition to a higher cost-of-capital environment.
• **Restricted Liquidity:** $601M in restricted cash held specifically for litigation settlements (primarily UK and U.S. merchant interchange disputes), representing a persistent drag on capital flexibility.
• **Operational Pivot:** Management utilized "expect" (high confidence) regarding the continued integration of Dynamic Yield and the expansion of open banking, while "anticipate" was used for broader macroeconomic stabilization, indicating a more cautious outlook on consumer credit health.
