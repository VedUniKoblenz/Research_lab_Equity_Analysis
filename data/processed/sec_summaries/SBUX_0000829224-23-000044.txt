A pivot in R&D/CapEx is evident through the "Siren System" rollout, aimed at reducing "out-of-the-way" tasks, signaling a shift from product-centric R&D to process-engineering headcount optimization.

• **Capital Allocation Signals:** Buyback pace accelerated to $655M in Q3 from $464M in Q2, totaling $1.7B for the first nine months. Dividend payout remains fixed at $0.53/share, but interest expense surged to $145M (+23% YoY), driven by higher rates on variable debt, potentially limiting further buyback expansion without additional leverage.
