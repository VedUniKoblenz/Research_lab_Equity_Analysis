• **Segment KPI & Trajectory**: Semiconductor Solutions revenue reached $5.87B (+20% YoY), significantly outpacing Infrastructure Software at $1.83B (+5% YoY). Total Software RPO (Remaining Performance Obligations) stands at $15.2B, providing high-visibility coverage for the next 12 months.

• **Hidden Operational Efficiency**: Inventory levels held flat at $1.29B vs. Oct 2021 despite a 16% YoY revenue increase, signaling a "hand-to-mouth" supply chain with zero margin for error but exceptionally high asset turnover. 
• **FY22 Restructuring Signal**: Formal initiation of a "FY22 Restructuring Plan" involving specific employee severance and lease exit costs, suggesting a push for margin expansion in the software segment following the CA/Symantec integration tail-ends.
• **Debt Refinancing Velocity**: Redeemed $2.31B of senior notes during the quarter. Management pivot toward aggressive deleveraging and lower interest expense ($415M vs $486M YoY) despite the high-rate environment.
• **Legal Overhang Nuance**: Material shift in Caltech litigation; while the $1.1B judgment was vacated, the case is remanded for a new trial on damages, creating a persistent, unquantified liability "above $0 but potentially below $1.1B."
• **Forward Guidance**: Management shifted to high-confidence "We expect" language for Q2 revenue of ~$7.9B, implying 20% YoY growth, a deliberate signal of backlog strength and supply chain visibility extending through FY22.
