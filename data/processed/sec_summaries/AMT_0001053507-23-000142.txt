• **Operational Pivot/Disposals**: Executed strategic exits from non-core assets including Mexico Fiber (closed 03/29/23) and Poland operations (closed 05/31/23). Signals aggressive portfolio pruning to optimize the balance sheet against high-interest debt.
• **Service Revenue Divergence**: Services revenue (site application/installation) contracted 15.7% YoY to $43M (vs. $51M in Q2 2022). This leading indicator contradicts management's bullish 5G narratives, suggesting a tangible cooling in carrier CAPEX/deployment velocity.
• **Segment Trajectory (Data Centers)**: Data Center revenue reached $213M (+9.2% YoY), outstripping organic tower growth. Segment reliance on Stonepeak Partners (29% minority interest) creates a complex structural claim on cash flows despite high top-line performance.
• **Regional Concentration Risk**: Asia-Pacific revenue ($253M) remains heavily indexed to Indian market volatility. Specifically, the "Indian Customer" (VIL) receivables profile remains a primary threat to Adjusted EBITDA and RPO stability.
• **Financing Shifts**: Heavy utilization of the 2021 Multicurrency Credit Facility and specific EUR Three-Year Delayed Draw Term Loans. Refinancing focus has shifted toward the European debt market to leverage localized rate differentials.
• **Capital Allocation**: Total property revenue grew to $2,729M, but net income is increasingly impacted by the $10.7B+ CoreSite-related debt service. No material change in share buyback pace, signaling priority on debt-to-EBITDA deleveraging over immediate equity returns.
