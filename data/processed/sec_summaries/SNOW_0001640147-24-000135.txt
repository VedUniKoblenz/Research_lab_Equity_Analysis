• **Net Revenue Retention (NRR) Decay:** DBNRR fell to 128% from 151% YoY. While management maintains a bullish long-term outlook on the "AI Data Cloud," the 2,300 bps compression indicates a rapid deceleration in existing account expansion that contradicts the "stable consumption" narrative.
• **Aggressive Buyback Acceleration:** Repurchased $516.4M of Class A common stock (3.1M shares) in Q1 FY25 alone, representing ~87% of the total $591M repurchased in the entire previous fiscal year. This aggressive pace indicates an urgent attempt to support the stock price following the CEO transition and NRR cooling.
• **RPO Duration Shift:** Total RPO reached $5.0B (+46% YoY), but the portion expected to be recognized as revenue within 24 months has shifted. Current RPO ($2.5B) is now exactly 50% of total RPO, suggesting management is securing longer-term, multi-year commitments to mask near-term consumption volatility.
• **Operational Pivot to AI/Iceberg:** Capitalized internal-use software costs increased to $33.4M. Strategic R&D spend is pivoting heavily toward "Snowflake Horizon" and "Cortex AI," marking a deprecation of legacy "storage-first" features in favor of compute-heavy AI services.
• **Atypical Risk - Credential Exploitation:** Filing includes a specific, non-boilerplate disclosure regarding "unauthorized access to certain customer accounts" using stolen credentials. Management flags potential material impact on consumption if customers reduce data exposure or migration velocity due to perceived security risks.
• **High-Value Customer Concentration:** Customers with >$1M in 12-month product revenue hit 485 (+30% YoY), but the growth rate in this segment is trailing the 46% RPO growth, suggesting large contract "shelfware" risk where commitments exceed actual utilization.
• **Guidance Nuance:** Management used "we expect" (high confidence) for the $3.30B FY25 product revenue target, but "we believe" (speculative) regarding the timing of GenAI-related revenue contributions, signaling that AI is not yet a material driver of the current $790M quarterly product revenue.
