• **Fuel Under-Recovery Alpha:** Massive spike in "Fuel and purchased power" expense (+$642M YoY). Under-recovered fuel costs (regulatory assets) at Georgia Power and Alabama Power increased by >$450M in Q1 alone. This creates a significant "hidden" cash flow drag and potential future rate-case friction not fully priced into the yield.

XBRL data confirms 15% ($660M) is expected to be recognized within the next 12 months, primarily in Southern Power’s wholesale segment, providing high-visibility but limited growth upside in a rising-rate environment.

• **Interest Rate Sensitivity:** Debt portfolio contains $4.8B in variable-rate exposure. A 100bps move in rates is modeled to impact annual pre-tax earnings by ~$48M. Recent Series 2021B Junior Subordinated Notes (1.875%) and Southern Power Senior Notes (1.000%) reflect "peak" cheap financing that cannot be replicated for upcoming maturities.
