• **Capital Allocation Deceleration:** Significant slowdown in share repurchases; Q2 2023 buybacks totaled 1.1M shares ($386M) vs. Q1 2023 ($1.0B) and Q4 2022 ($1.1B). Shift signals pivot from equity shrinkage to internal CapEx for rural expansion.

• **Balance Sheet Evolution:** Rapid growth in "Equipment Installment Plan" (EIP) receivables (Context c-185/c-186) indicates CHTR is increasingly acting as a consumer lender for mobile handsets, introducing credit risk and working capital drag atypical for cable models.

• **Video Revenue Erosion:** Residential Video net losses of 189k; higher programming costs (Context c-149) and "cord-cutting" are compressing segment margins, despite Residential Internet ARPU growth of ~4% YoY.
• **Debt Technicals:** Total debt principal of $97.6B. Weighted average interest rate at 5.4%. Increased reliance on "Senior Secured Notes" (Context c-91) to refinance floating-rate credit facilities (Context c-95) amid rising rate environment.
• **Share Count Technicals:** Class A Common Stock outstanding reduced to 149,670,941 (Context c-2) from 152,651,396 at year-end 2022 (Context c-6), a 1.95% net reduction in H1 2023.
