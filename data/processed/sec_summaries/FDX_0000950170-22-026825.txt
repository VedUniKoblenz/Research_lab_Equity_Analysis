• **Operational Pivot:** Management accelerated cost-reduction targets to $3.7B for FY23 (previously $2.2B–$2.7B range), signaling an aggressive shift from growth-at-all-costs to margin defense. Key levers: 6% reduction in flight hours and 9% reduction in international flight frequencies.
• **Segment KPI Divergence:** 
  - **Express:** Revenue -6% YoY; total package volume -12%. Yield +8% failed to offset volume deleveraging.
  - **Ground:** Revenue +2% YoY despite -9% volume contraction; growth entirely supported by +13% yield expansion via fuel surcharges and base rate hikes. 
  - **Freight:** Operating margin expansion to 17.8% (up 310 bps YoY), driven by +18% yield per shipment.
• **Capital Allocation:** $1.5B Accelerated Share Repurchase (ASR) completed in Q2 FY23. Total FY23 buyback target remains $1.5B, implying a pause in repurchases for the remainder of the fiscal year.
• **Forward-Looking Nuance:** Management "expects" (high confidence) $1B in incremental cost savings in 2H FY23 but only "believes" (speculative) that global demand will stabilize, indicating internal reliance on expense control rather than macro recovery.
• **Risk Escalation:** New emphasis on Ground segment "purchased transportation" costs (+130 bps impact) and contractor network stability. Regulatory exposure updated regarding European VAT and international customs compliance.
• **Guidance:** Adjusted EPS range narrowed/confirmed at $13.00–$14.00, contingent on achieving $2.5B in structural DRIVE cost reductions. Cash flow from operations "anticipated" at $4B–$5B.
