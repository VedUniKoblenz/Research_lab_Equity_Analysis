• **Purchase Commitments:** Despite "asset-light" messaging, significant unrecorded purchase obligations remain for Boeing 777F and 767F aircraft through FY26/FY27, limiting CAPEX flexibility if global trade volumes further deteriorate.
• **Debt Structure:** Active management of Euro-denominated notes (including 0.450% due 2025 and 1.625% due 2027) highlights an ongoing strategy to hedge international revenue streams via structural currency matching in the debt stack.
