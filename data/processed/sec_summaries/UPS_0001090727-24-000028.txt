• **Sentiment Divergence:** Management reaffirms FY24 revenue guidance of $92.0B–$94.5B and adj. operating margin of 10.0%–10.6% despite a 5.3% Q1 revenue contraction ($21.7B). This implies an aggressive 2H recovery and significant margin expansion from the current Q1 consol. adj. margin of 8.0%.

• **Hidden Operational Shifts:** Workforce reduction underway targeting 12,000 management positions; Q1 restructuring charges totaled $110M. UPS is pivoting from "volume-at-any-cost" to a "Better, not Bigger" framework, specifically exploring the divestiture of Coyote Logistics (SCS segment) to reduce asset intensity and volatility.

• **Forward-Looking Nuance:** "We expect" (high confidence) FY24 CapEx of ~$4.5B and dividend payments of ~$5.4B. Management "anticipates" U.S. Domestic volume growth to turn positive in 2H24, a pivot point from the Q1 ADV (Average Daily Volume) decline of 3.2%.

• **Segment-Specific KPIs:**

  - **International:** ADV down 5.8% YoY; Adj. operating margin compressed to 15.4% (vs 18.2% YoY).
  - **SCS:** Revenue $3.2B (-7.0%); Logistics sub-segment showed resilience, but Forwarding was dragged by market overcapacity.

• **Capital Allocation:** Reauthorized $5.0B share repurchase program. Dividend remains a priority with $1.63/share paid in Q1 ($1.3B total), representing a high payout ratio against Q1 GAAP EPS of $1.30.

• **Debt & Liquidity:** Cash and marketable securities at $5.2B, down from $5.7B at FYE23. Significant upcoming maturities: 1.625% Senior Notes due 2025. Current debt-to-adj. EBITDA remains within target, but interest expense rose to $225M in Q1.
