This masks underlying commodity volatility and shifts reported gross margins, making "crack spread" analysis based on headline numbers less accurate without normalizing for these embedded hedges.

• **Hidden Operational Risk:** Escalated exposure to Central Mexico Terminals via VIE structures; regulatory/political shifts in Mexican energy policy now directly impact consolidated asset valuation in the "Corporate/Other" reconciliations.
