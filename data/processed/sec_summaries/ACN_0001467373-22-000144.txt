• **Record Booking Velocity:** New bookings reached a terminal high of $19.6B (+22% YoY), yielding a robust 1.3x book-to-bill ratio. Consulting bookings led at $10.9B (56% of total), signaling a pivot toward high-margin discretionary spend vs. defensive outsourcing ($8.7B).
• **Aggressive Capacity Expansion:** Total headcount surged to 699,000, a ~33% YoY increase. This massive labor absorption suggests management anticipates a sustained multi-year demand cycle, overriding immediate concerns of a macro slowdown.
• **Guidance Inflection:** Management raised FY22 revenue growth guidance to 24%–26% (local currency) from the prior 19%–22% range. The phrase "we expect" is used with high confidence regarding market share gains in Cloud and Security.
• **Margin-Cost Tension:** Operating margin narrowed slightly to 13.7% in Q2 due to "higher recruitment and compensation costs." Management is betting on pricing power and increased "off-shore" utilization to neutralize 200–300 bps of wage inflation pressure.
• **Capital Allocation Acceleration:** Share repurchases totaled $1.1B in Q2 (up from $835M in Q2-21). H1 M&A spend reached $1.2B across 20+ "individually immaterial" acquisitions, indicating a strategy of "tuck-in" technical talent acquisition over large-scale integration risk.
• **Geographic KPI Trajectory:** Revenue in North America ($7.1B, +26%) and Growth Markets ($2.9B, +28%) outpaced Europe ($5.0B, +25%), reflecting a higher concentration of "digital-first" transformation deals in the US and Asia.
• **Hidden Risk/Asset Shift:** Exited Russian operations (2,300 personnel) post-quarter; while financially immaterial (<0.5% revenue), it signals a material "S" in ESG risk-management that may impact delivery capacity in the CEE region.
