Increased granularity in billing status (Unbilled vs. Billed vs. Past Due) indicates defensive monitoring of the consumer credit cycle as device ASPs remain high.

• **Spectrum Drag:** Ongoing "projected clearing costs" for C-Band spectrum licenses continue to impact cash flow, with indefinite-lived wireless licenses remaining the dominant, non-amortizing asset, creating a high-sensitivity point for future impairment tests if interest rates remain elevated.
