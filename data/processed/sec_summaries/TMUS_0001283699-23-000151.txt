• **Regulatory Friction:** "Technology Obsolescence" risk materially modified regarding 2,500 MHz Auction 108 licenses. TMUS paid $304M but FCC has not granted licenses due to authority expiration; results in localized capacity constraints in rural/suburban clusters.

• **Debt Profile:** Net debt includes $73.3B in long-term notes. Recent issuances (Feb/May 2023) at 4.800%–5.650% suggest rising cost of carry for the $3.5B–$5.0B annual refinancing requirements over the next 36 months.
