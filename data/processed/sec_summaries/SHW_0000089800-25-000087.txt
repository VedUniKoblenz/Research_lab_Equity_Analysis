This inorganic push suggests a hedge against stagnant North American DIY/residential paint volumes.

Increased reliance on Domestic and Foreign Commercial Paper programs for bridge financing indicates potential margin compression from higher interest carry.

Environmental remediation reserves are increasingly concentrated in New Jersey (Major Sites), suggesting localized regulatory pressure and potential under-accrual for long-tail liabilities.

• **Geographic Concentration Risk:** Despite EMEA/International M&A, revenue remains disproportionately tied to North American Paint Stores Group (PSG). Management's continued use of 'Net Investment Hedging' for Euro/USD cross-currency swaps (EuroCrossCurrencySwap 1-5) flags heightened sensitivity to foreign exchange volatility as EMEA footprint scales.
