• **Capital Allocation Acceleration:** New $2.5B share repurchase authorization approved Feb 2023, following a separate $2.5B authorization in Oct 2022. Rapid succession of authorizations indicates aggressive pivot toward capital return over capacity expansion.
• **Deleveraging Signal:** Redeemed $350M in total senior notes in Feb 2023, specifically targeting high-coupon and long-dated paper: 6.625% (due 2037), 3.650% (due 2051), and 4.000% (due 2052). Focus on retiring debt with 14-29 year maturities signals a move to insulate the balance sheet from long-term rate volatility.
• **Renewable Diesel (DGD) Hyper-growth:** Segment revenue doubled YoY to $1.4B in Q1 2023 (vs. $0.7B in Q1 2022). High-margin Renewable Naphtha sales emerged as a meaningful contributor within the segment, though specific volume splits remain opaque.
• **Refining Mix Shift:** Distillate revenue grew to $14.9B (+10.4% YoY) while Gasoline and Blendstocks fell to $15.8B (-6.5% YoY). Management is successfully capturing the distillate/gasoline crack spread divergence, offsetting volume weakness in consumer gasoline.
• **Liquidity/VIE Capacity:** Diamond Green Diesel (DGD) VIE maintained a $400M revolving credit facility with $0 drawn as of March 31, 2023. Self-funding status of the JV reduces Valero’s consolidated cash call risk for renewable expansion.
• **Operational Expense Nuance:** Foreign excise taxes held steady at $1.422B, suggesting stable international throughput despite macroeconomic headwinds in European/Atlantic markets.
• **Inventory/Supply Chain:** Renewable Diesel inventory valuation appears sensitive to feedstock price volatility, yet segment operating income expansion suggests strong pass-through of LCFS (Low Carbon Fuel Standard) credit values.
