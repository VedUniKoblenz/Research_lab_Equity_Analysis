**Senior Equity Research Analyst: NFLX Q2 2024 Alpha Extraction**

*   **Sentiment/Data Contradiction:** Management tone remains highly bullish on "long-term ad revenue," yet the 10-Q reveals "advertising is not yet a primary driver of revenue growth." Operating margin expansion to 27.2% was driven by timing of content spend/marketing, not ad-revenue efficiency.

*   **Risk Escalation:** Materially modified risk language regarding "evolving regulatory landscapes" in international markets (EU/LATAM) concerning local content quotas and "levy" taxes, posing a threat to margin parity across regions.
