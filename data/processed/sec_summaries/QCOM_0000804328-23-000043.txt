Divergence suggests a pivot to AI sentiment to mask the fundamental erosion in mid-to-low-tier handset volumes.

• **Forward-Looking Nuance:** Management uses "expect" (high confidence) regarding continued macroeconomic headwinds and OEM inventory drawdowns through calendar year-end. Use of "anticipate" (moderate confidence) for a recovery in China demand, signaling uncertainty in the region. Q4 FY23 revenue guidance midpoint of $8.5B implies a sharp YoY contraction, confirming the delayed "second-half recovery."

• **Atypical Risk Factors:** Material escalation in China concentration risk; 60% of total revenue is tied to China-based customers. New emphasis on US export control modifications (specifically July 2023 updates) potentially impacting future shipments of AI-integrated SoCs and high-performance computing nodes.
