This transitions the credit book to a capital-light model, materially reducing idiosyncratic credit risk and freeing up ~$1.8B in cash at closing to support the buyback program.

• **Unbranded vs. Branded Trajectory:** Braintree/Unbranded TPV continues to grow at ~30%+, while high-margin "PayPal Branded Checkout" remains stagnant/low-single digits. Quantitative risk: Every 100bps shift from branded to unbranded reduces net take rate by approximately 1.5-2.0bps.

Efficiency gains are being reinvested into "Next Gen Checkout" and AI, signaling a pivot point where R&D is now focused on defensive moat-building rather than top-line expansion.
