• **Sanofi Profit Explosion:** Collaboration revenue surged 36% to $1.40B, driven by an $898M share of Sanofi collaboration profits (+45% YoY). This reflects massive Dupixent scaling and a decreasing proportional R&D reimbursement burden. Management's tone on Dupixent COPD sBLA remains "expectant" (high confidence) for 2024.

• **Oncology Pivot Spend:** R&D expense reached $1.15B (+20% YoY). Spend is pivoting away from late-stage Dupixent trials toward costimulatory bispecifics. Management uses "expect" regarding 2024 FDA action dates for Odronextamab (NHL) and Linvoseltamab (MM), signaling high confidence in diversifying away from ophthalmology.

• **Capital Allocation:** Repurchased $397M of stock in Q3 at an average price of $814.77/share. YTD repurchases total $1.34B. $14.2B in cash/marketable securities remains largely un-deployed, suggesting dry powder for potential M&A to bolster the mid-stage immunology pipeline.

• **Concentration Risk:** Gross product sales remain hyper-concentrated; Besse Medical and McKesson Corp account for 38% and 31% of gross revenue, respectively. Any shift in distributor terms or inventory management at these two nodes represents a 69% tail-risk to net product sales.
