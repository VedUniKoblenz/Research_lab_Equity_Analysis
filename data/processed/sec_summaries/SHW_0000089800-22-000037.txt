• **Sentiment Divergence:** Management maintains "high confidence" in The Americas Group (TAG) momentum despite 30-year mortgage rates exceeding 7% during the period, signaling a potential disconnect between lag-time pro-paint demand and immediate housing turnover deceleration. Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO) expansion suggests a pivot from "just-in-time" to "just-in-case" safety stocking, increasing margin risk if raw material costs (up high-teens YoY) deflate rapidly.

  - PCG: Net Sales +24.1%; impacted by 5.5% acquisition tailwind (Specialty Polymers) and 6.1% FX headwind.
