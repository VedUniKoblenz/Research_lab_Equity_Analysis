• **Segment Risk Concentration:** Note 2 explicitly flags a new revenue concentration risk in "Other Segment" (SRS) specifically tied to "Roofing Products." This marks a departure from the traditional balanced retail product mix of the "Primary Segment."
• **Proactive Refinancing:** On May 6, 2025 (post-quarter), HD executed a new $3.5B five-year revolving credit facility expiring May 2030, replacing the July 2027 facility 26 months early. This proactive reset of the liquidity ladder suggests a strategic lock-in of credit capacity ahead of anticipated macro-tightening.
• **Asset Quality Shift:** Total Goodwill and Intangible Assets now reflect the full weight of the SRS acquisition ($18.25B+ impact); the pivot from organic retail to an inorganic, distribution-led model is complete, shifting the valuation floor from tangible book to cash flow multiples.
• **Inventory/Restructuring:** While not detailed in the summary, the high CP usage despite the $2.55B debt pay-down implies a leaner cash position than historical averages, likely to drive higher interest expense in Q2.
