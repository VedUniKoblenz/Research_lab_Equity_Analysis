Signal: Shift from share buybacks ($1.6B YTD vs. $2.0B+ in prior comparable periods) to inorganic growth in "Megatrends" (Automation, Aviation, Energy).

Technical signal: Management is intentionally over-stocking in Aerospace to mitigate persistent Tier 2/3 supplier fragility in castings and forgings, diverging from standard "Honeywell Accelerator" lean principles.
• **Guidance Revision Nuance:** FY24 Sales guidance revised upward to $39.1B–$39.7B (prior $38.1B–$38.9B) but Segment Margin ceiling lowered 10bps to 23.7%. "We expect" (high confidence) organic growth of 5-6%, but "anticipate" (moderate confidence) continued headwinds in Industrial Automation (Productivity Products organic sales down mid-single digits).
• **Restructuring Signal:** $116M in Q2 restructuring charges, primarily for severance and asset impairments in Industrial Automation. Indicates more aggressive headcount/footprint reduction than previously signaled to protect margins in a weak short-cycle warehouse/automation recovery.
